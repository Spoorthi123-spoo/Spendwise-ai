## SpendWise AI Context

SpendWise AI is a SaaS platform that audits AI software spending for startups and teams.

The application helps users:

- Discover unnecessary AI subscriptions
- Reduce monthly AI costs
- Compare AI plans
- View annual savings opportunities
- Receive optimization recommendations
